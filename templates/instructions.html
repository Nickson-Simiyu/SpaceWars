Space Wars - Instructions

How to Play

Welcome to Space Wars! Here are the instructions on how to play:

  1. Use the arrow keys to control the red spaceship.
  2. Use the A, W, S, and D keys to control the yellow spaceship.
  3. Press the LCTRL key to shoot bullets from the yellow spaceship.
  4. Press the RCTRL key to shoot bullets from the red spaceship.
  5. Avoid getting hit by your opponent's bullets.
  6. Keep an eye on your health, and be the last spaceship standing to win!
  7. To exit the game, press the Escape key.
